## What is Luma Dream Machine?

## Key Features in 2026

### 1. Exceptional Prompt Adherence
Luma has always been known for understanding exactly what the user wants. Unlike earlier models that often ignored complex instructions, Dream Machine excels at following specific directorial prompts, such as "low-angle tracking shot of a futuristic city at sunset."

### 2. Temporal Consistency
One of the biggest hurdles in AI video is "morphing" — where objects change shape or disappear between frames. Luma’s 2026 updates have virtually eliminated this, making it one of the few tools capable of producing 10-second clips that look like they were shot on a physical camera.

### 3. Realistic Physics
From the way water splashes to how fabric moves in the wind, Dream Machine’s physics engine is remarkably accurate. This makes it a go-to for creators working on cinematic projects or high-end social media content.
