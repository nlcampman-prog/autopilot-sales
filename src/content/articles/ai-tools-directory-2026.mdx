**Best for:** Speed, short-form copy  
**Pricing:** $36/mo (free tier available)

## How to Choose the Right AI Tool

1. **Start with your workflow** — What specific task takes you the most time each week? Automate that first.
2. **Use free trials** — Most tools offer 7-14 day trials. Don't commit until you've tested it on your actual data.
3. **Check integrations** — Ensure the tool connects with your existing tech stack (Slack, Google Drive, etc.).
4. **Read our in-depth reviews** — We have deep dives on almost all these tools right here on [autopilotsales.org](/articles).
5. **Start small** — Don't try to adopt 10 tools at once. Master one tool, see the ROI, then add the next.
