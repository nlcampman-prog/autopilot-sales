## What is Relevance AI?

Relevance AI is a low-code platform specifically designed for building and deploying AI agents at scale. Unlike general-purpose AI chat interfaces, Relevance provides a structured environment where you can define an agent’s tools, knowledge base, and specific workflow steps.

Whether you need an agent to monitor your competitors, manage your lead generation, or automate your customer support, Relevance AI offers the building blocks to make it happen without requiring a degree in computer science.

## Key Features of the Platform

### 1. Agent Builder
The core of the platform is the Agent Builder, which uses a "flow-based" approach. You can drag and drop different "tools" (like Google Search, web scraping, or API calls) into an agent's workflow. This allows the agent to interact with the real world rather than just relying on its internal training data.

### 2. The Tool Builder
Relevance allows you to create custom "tools" that your agents can use. For example, you can build a tool that specifically scrapes data from LinkedIn or one that pulls specific financial data from an internal API. Once built, these tools can be shared across your entire agent workforce.

### 3. High-Volume Execution
One of Relevance AI’s biggest strengths in 2026 is its ability to run thousands of tasks in parallel. If you have a list of 5,000 companies you need to research, you can deploy your agents to handle them all simultaneously, a task that would take a human weeks to complete.

### 4. Seamless Integrations
Relevance connects natively with the tools your business already uses:
*   **Slack & Discord**: For interactive agents.
*   **Google Workspace**: For managing emails, sheets, and docs.
*   **Zapier & Make**: For connecting to thousands of other apps.
