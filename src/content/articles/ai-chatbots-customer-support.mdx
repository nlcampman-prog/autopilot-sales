## How to Train Your AI Support Bot
The "Garbage In, Garbage Out" rule still applies in 2026. To get an 80% resolution rate, follow these steps:
1. **Clean Your Docs:** Before connecting your help center, remove outdated articles. The bot will believe whatever you tell it.
2. **Review "Unanswered" Questions:** Every week, look at what the bot couldn't solve. Write one new help article to cover that gap.
3. **Set the Tone:** Most 2026 bots allow you to choose a persona (Helpful, Professional, Quirky). Match it to your brand voice.
