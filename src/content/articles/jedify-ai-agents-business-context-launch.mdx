## The Problem: AI Agents Without a Brain

Until now, deploying AI agents often resulted in a "hallucination headache." You could give an agent access to your CRM, but it wouldn't know the nuance of a high-priority client or the cultural context of a specific department's workflow.

Standard RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) solutions like early LangChain or LlamaIndex implementations provided the data, but not the *reasoning* behind the data.

### Why Context Matters
- **Customer Support:** An agent needs to know the difference between a "standard" delay and a "catastrophic" one based on previous ticket history.
- **Internal Ops:** AI needs to understand the "unwritten rules" of how your specific team prioritizes tasks.
- **Sales:** Knowing a lead's history across three different platforms is essential for a personalized touch.

## The Jedify Solution: Contextual Data Integration

Jedify isn't just another vector database connector. It is a **Business Context Layer**. It sits between your LLM (whether it's Claude 5, GPT-6, or Gemini 3) and your company's data stack.

### How Jedify Works

1. **Auto-Discovery:** Jedify crawls your internal tools (Slack, Notion, Jira, Salesforce) and uses "Context Mapping" to identify relationships between people, projects, and policies.
2. **Dynamic Context Injection:** When an agent receives a prompt, Jedify doesn't just find relevant text; it assembles a "Context Packet" that includes the sentiment, hierarchy, and historical relevance of the data.
3. **Continuous Learning:** As your team works, Jedify observes how humans resolve issues and updates its context map in real-time.
