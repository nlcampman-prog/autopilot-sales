# 7 Best AI Video Editing & Creation Tools in 2024 (Ranked)

The barrier to entry for high-quality video production has never been lower. Just a few years ago, creating professional-grade video required expensive hardware, complex software like Adobe Premiere Pro, and years of specialized training. Today, **Artificial Intelligence** has turned that model on its head.

In 2024, AI video tools can handle everything from automatic captioning and background removal to generating entire scenes from a single text prompt. Whether you're a YouTuber, a social media marketer, or a business owner, these tools allow you to produce more content, in less time, with zero technical expertise.

Here are the 7 best AI video editing and creation tools ranked for 2024.

## 2. InVideo (Best for Fast Text-to-Video)

If you have a script or a blog post and need to turn it into a video instantly, **InVideo** is the industry leader. It features an incredibly intuitive text-to-video workflow and a massive library of over 8 million stock assets.

### Key AI Features:
*   **AI Video Generator:** Just type a prompt, and InVideo AI generates a complete video with script, voiceover, and media.
*   **Intelligent Stock Selection:** The AI automatically matches your script with the best high-quality stock footage.
*   **AI Voiceovers:** Choose from hundreds of human-like AI voices in various accents.

**Best For:** Content marketers, faceless YouTube channels, and small business ads.

## 3. HeyGen (Best for AI Avatars)

**HeyGen** is the gold standard for AI avatars. Instead of hiring a spokesperson or getting on camera yourself, you can use a digital "Lindy" to deliver your message with perfect lip-syncing and expression.

### Key AI Features:

*   **Corporate Utility:** Perfect for training videos, personalized sales outreach, and global marketing.

**Best For:** Corporate training, sales teams, and international brands.

## 4. Runway (Best for Creative Effects)

**Runway** is the frontier of generative AI video. It’s less of a traditional editor and more of a "magic wand" for filmmakers and creative directors.

### Key AI Features:
*   **Gen-2 (Text-to-Video):** Generate entirely new video clips from text prompts.
*   **Motion Brush:** Paint an area of a static image to make it move realistically.
*   **AI Magic Tools:** Inpainting (remove objects), Infinite Image (expand backgrounds), and Frame Interpolation.

**Best For:** Experimental filmmakers and high-end creative agencies.

---

## 5. Descript (Best for Podcast & Interview Editing)

**Descript** treats video editing like a Word document. Instead of looking at a complex timeline, you edit the transcript of your video. If you delete a sentence in the text, the corresponding video clip is deleted.

### Key AI Features:
*   **Studio Sound:** Use AI to make a recording on a cheap microphone sound like it was done in a professional studio.
*   **Overdub:** Create a text-to-speech clone of your own voice to fix "ums" or change a line without re-recording.
*   **Filler Word Removal:** Remove every "um," "uh," and "like" from your video with one click.

**Best For:** Podcasters, interviewers, and webinar hosts.

---

## 6. Synthesia (Best for Enterprise Training)

**Synthesia** was a pioneer in the AI video space and remains the top choice for large-scale corporate environments. It focuses on turning text into professional talking-head videos for internal communication.

### Key AI Features:
*   **Professional Avatars:** High-fidelity avatars that look and sound extremely corporate-professional.
*   **Massive Language Support:** 120+ languages and accents.
*   **Template Library:** Pre-built scenes for tutorials, sales pitches, and company updates.

**Best For:** HR departments and large-scale educational platforms.

---

## 7. Fliki (Best for Social Media Automation)

**Fliki** combines text-to-speech and text-to-video into one simple platform. It is designed for "Social Media First" creators who want to turn tweets or blog posts into viral clips.

### Key AI Features:
*   **Blog-to-Video:** Automatically summarize a blog post and turn it into a video with subtitles.
*   **AI Voice Cloning:** Rapidly clone your voice for consistent branding.
*   **Twitter-to-Video:** Turn a thread into a video formatted for TikTok or X.

**Best For:** Bloggers and social media managers looking for pure volume.
