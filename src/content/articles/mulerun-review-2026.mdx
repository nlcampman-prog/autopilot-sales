## What is MuleRun?

MuleRun is a B2B AI platform designed to build and manage autonomous digital workers. These aren't just LLM wrappers; they are task-oriented agents capable of interacting with the web, managing files, and executing complex workflows across multiple software platforms.

The core USP of MuleRun is its **"Always-On" philosophy**. While most AI tools wait for a prompt, MuleRun agents can be scheduled to monitor markets, analyze stocks, or reconcile invoices continuously, providing you with results only when a specific condition is met or a task is completed.

## Key Features of MuleRun

### 1. Autonomous Reasoning Engines
MuleRun agents are built on top of the latest reasoning models (like Claude 3.5 Sonnet and GPT-4o), allowing them to break down high-level instructions (e.g., "Find all retail prospects in the UK with over $10M in revenue and draft a personalized outreach email") into a series of actionable steps without human intervention.

### 2. Deep Tool Integration & Dedicated Environments
One of MuleRun’s standout features is that each agent operates in its own secure, dedicated cloud environment. This allows the agents to handle sensitive data, interact with complex web dashboards, and even perform tasks like PPT generation or video rendering without taxing your local machine.

### 3. Collective Intelligence Network
MuleRun features a library of pre-built agent "skills" contributed by a global network of developers. Whether you need an agent for market research, stock analysis, or social media management, you can often find a template that gets you 90% of the way there.

## MuleRun Pricing 2026

MuleRun offers pricing based on "Active Agent Hours," allowing businesses to scale their costs alongside their output:

- **Starter:** $149/month. Includes 2 active agents and 50 agent hours.
- **Professional:** $499/month. Includes 10 active agents and 250 agent hours.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing. Unlimited agents, dedicated compute resources, and advanced security features.

While the price point is higher than a standard ChatGPT subscription, the ROI comes from the hours of human labor saved by having a dedicated digital worker.
